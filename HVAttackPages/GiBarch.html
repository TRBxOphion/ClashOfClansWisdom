GiBarch Attack Strategy

GiBarch

This attack strategy is very common at the lower Town Hall levels. It is an expansion of the Barch attack strategy with the addition of giants. It utilizes the higher hit-points of the giants to take out key defenses and to protect the barbarians and archers.

Army Composition

  • 6-8 Giants
  • 10-15 Barbarians
  • 15-25 Archers
  • 3-6 Wall breakers(optional)
  • CC: Giants

How to Perform

  1. Identify splash defenses such as mortars or wizard towers
  2. Use your giants and some of your wall breakers to take out these splash defenses.
