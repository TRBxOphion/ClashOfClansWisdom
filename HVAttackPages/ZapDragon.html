Zap Dragon Attack Strategy

Zap Dragon

The Zap Dragon strategy is very popular throughout many town halls. It is very easy to perform and thus many players use it. It can be used to push trophies or to farm. The only downside, is that the time to train dragons is a bit long.

Army Composition

  • Dragons
  • Lightning Spells
  • CC: Dragon 1 or/and balloons

How to Perform

  1. Identify the airdefenses, sweepers and other air targeting defenses.
  2. If there are heros like the archer queen close to an airdefense, wait until you can hit them with the lightning spells at the same time.
  3. Take out as many airdefenses, sweepers and other air targeting defenses with your lightning spells.
  4. If you didn't take out the sweepers, identify the remaining ones.
  5. Make the funnel on one side with your King and on the other with one dragon.
  6. Once the funnel is good enough send in your remaining dragons to wipe the base.
